Our Philosophy of Beauty

Founded in 2008, Éclat has redefined luxury beauty experiences in Sri Lanka. We believe true beauty comes from expert care combined with personalized attention.

Our team of internationally trained stylists and aestheticians continuously update their skills to bring you the latest techniques and trends in hair, skin, and nail care.

Certified Professionals: All our stylists undergo rigorous training and certification

Premium Products: We use only top-tier professional brands

Hygiene First: Strict sanitation protocols for your safety

Our Mission

To empower individuals through transformative beauty experiences that enhance confidence and celebrate individuality.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Sri Lanka's most innovative beauty destination, setting industry standards for excellence and client care.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices, from biodegradable products to energy-efficient equipment and recycling programs.
